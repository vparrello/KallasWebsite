Who Am I?

Hello! My name is Christ Anton Kallas II and I grew up in an entrepreneurial household that taught me the importance of bringing meaningful value to everything that I do.


Early Life - My mom ran the marketing strategy for my dad’s successful chocolate manufacturer & storefront. Since I was little, I have been acutely aware of the dramatic impact of seemingly simple business decisions.


Education - I loved the University of Illinois so much that I graduated twice, once in 2010 with an undergraduate degree in political science and the other was an MBA 12 years later.


Professional Life - I have over 10 years of marketing experience spearheading innovation, managing 360 media campaigns, setting brand strategy, copywriting, creating full-lifecycle product plans, leading agency IATs & research management, managing P&Ls, and so much more. I’ve worked with zero-year-old startups and multicentury old manufacturers; I’ve managed budgets of as little as $5,000 up to $50M.


No matter the size of your business, I have the solutions for you.
